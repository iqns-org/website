    - q: What do I get for free?

    - q: What is an ontology, practically speaking?
      a: A machine-readable vocabulary that maps standards, regulations, and institutional knowledge into a format AI agents and tools can query. Think of it as a shared dictionary your systems actually understand.
    - q: When should I upgrade to Business?
      a: When you need the semantic layer operationalised with a private knowledge server, managed MCP endpoint, or SPARQL hosting — so your team can focus on integration, not infrastructure.
    - q: Can I model internal policies alongside public standards?
      a: Yes. Business and Ecosystem plans include private knowledge bases where you can extend the public ontologies with your own internal standards, policies, and taxonomies — so shared organisational knowledge stays connected.
    - q: What are ITIL SKMS Connectors?
      a: They bridge IQ:NS ontologies to ITIL 4 Service Knowledge Management Systems — so your service desk, CMDB, and change management tools can tap into the same structured knowledge your AI agents use.
    - q: Is there a contract or commitment?
      a: No. Monthly plans, cancel anytime. Pay annually for 2 months free. Add-ons are one-time engagements scoped before you commit.
    - q: Where is my data hosted?
      a: Paid plans are deployed to your choice of AWS, GCP, or Azure — you pick the region. Private knowledge bases stay in your tenant. We never co-mingle customer data.
