# Industries & Domains

IQ:NS covers 20+  domains with 100+ ontology files. Each domain includes core vocabulary, framework alignments, and worked examples.

---

## AI & Machine Learning

30+ AI-specific frameworks including EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, ISO 23894, ISO 24027–29, OWASP LLM Top 10, MITRE ATLAS, Google SAIF, IEEE 7000, OECD AI Principles, and more. Platform examples for AWS, Azure, GCP, and Kubernetes.

## Financial Services

Basel III, DORA, MiFID II, SOX, SR 11-7, EBA AI guidelines. Structured for model risk management, regulatory reporting, and cross-border compliance.

## Healthcare

HIPAA, EU MDR, FDA AI/ML SaMD, ONC trustworthy AI. Modelled for clinical decision support, diagnostics, and medical device governance.

## Enterprise

COBIT, ISO 27001, ISO 31000, ISO 45001, SOC 2. Enterprise risk, information security, and occupational health — the backbone ontologies for organisational governance.

## Government

FedRAMP, NIST CSF, ISO 38500. Public sector frameworks for IT governance, cybersecurity, and cloud security.

## Defence

DoD Responsible AI Principles, NATO AI Principles. Responsible AI in defence and security contexts.

## Legal

UN Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights. Cross-domain AI-legal SKOS alignments.

## Insurance

Solvency II. Underwriting, claims processing, and pricing fairness ontologies.

## Education

UNESCO AI Ethics. Responsible AI in learning, assessment, and educational technology.

## Employment

EEOC, EU Equal Treatment Directive. Fairness in AI-driven hiring, screening, and workforce management.

## Energy

ISO 50001. Energy management systems and AI integration.

## Media

EU Digital Services Act. Content recommendation, platform governance, and algorithmic transparency.

## Public Safety

ISO 22320. Emergency management, surveillance governance, and public-interest AI.

## Transport

UN R157. Autonomous vehicles, automated driving systems, and vehicle safety.

## SaaS & SKMS

Service Knowledge Management System ontologies for AWS, Azure, GCP, and DigitalOcean. Maps cloud services to a shared vocabulary.

## ITIL

ITIL 4 service management ontology. Connects IT service management to the broader knowledge graph.

## Startups

UN Global Compact. Responsible AI foundations for early-stage companies.

## Cross-Domain

AI-legal SKOS alignments that bridge domain boundaries — connecting AI risk concepts to legal obligations across sectors.
