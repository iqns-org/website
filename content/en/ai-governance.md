IQ:NS provides a shared semantic foundation for AI governance, enabling organizations to map policies, controls, and risk across teams and systems.

- Model obligations, controls, and roles with stable identifiers
- Align regulatory requirements with operational practices
- Enable queryable governance intelligence for compliance and risk teams
