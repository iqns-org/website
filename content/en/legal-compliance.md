# IQ:NS for Legal & Compliance

## The challenge

Legal and compliance teams translate regulations into operational requirements across multiple jurisdictions and frameworks. AI introduces overlapping terminology, changing requirements, and parallel standards that resist manual synthesis.

## What the ontologies provide

- **Obligation profiling** — which frameworks apply to each AI system, with reasoned mappings from legal text to structured concepts
- **Cross-framework alignment** — see where GDPR, EU AI Act, NIST, and sector rules overlap or diverge
- **Change tracking** — when regulations update, the ontologies reflect what changed and what it affects
- **Shared vocabulary** — legal and engineering teams work from the same structured definitions

## How it fits

The ontologies don't replace legal judgement. They provide the structured foundation that makes interpretation consistent, auditable, and shareable across teams.
