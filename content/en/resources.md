## Ontology documentation

Each ontology ships with inline documentation — labels, definitions, scope notes, and source references. The Turtle files are self-describing.

## Framework guides

Quick references for the standards IQ:NS covers:

- **EU AI Act** — risk classification, transparency, conformity assessment
- **NIST AI RMF** — Govern, Map, Measure, Manage
- **ISO 42001** — management system approach, Annex A controls
- **GDPR AI provisions** — automated decisions, profiling, data rights
- **Sector standards** — FDA, FTC, EBA, SR 11-7, and more
