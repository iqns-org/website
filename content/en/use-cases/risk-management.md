# IQ:NS for Risk Management

Risk teams need to understand how requirements, controls, and obligations relate across frameworks, domains, and operational contexts. IQ:NS makes those relationships explicit and queryable.

## The challenge

Risk management is not just about mapping standards. It is about connecting those standards to real systems, data flows, and decision boundaries. Too often, frameworks remain siloed in spreadsheets and reports.

## What the ontologies provide

- **Control alignment** — see how one requirement connects to multiple frameworks and policies
- **Gap analysis** — identify where coverage is missing or inconsistent across domains
- **Contextual exposure** — assess risk based on jurisdiction, sector, and AI capability
- **Actionable insight** — drive monitoring, testing, and remediation with the same structured knowledge

## How it fits

Use IQ:NS with your risk taxonomy and reporting tools so every assessment is linked back to a shared organisational vocabulary. That makes risk faster to understand and easier to audit.
