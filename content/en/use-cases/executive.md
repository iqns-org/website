# IQ:NS for Executives

## The challenge

Executives own AI risk appetite and accountability. The questions are straightforward — what AI are we running, what are our obligations, do we have evidence — but the answers are scattered across teams, documents, and frameworks.

## What the ontologies provide

- **Obligation clarity** — which standards apply to your AI systems, structured and queryable
- **Cross-framework view** — one vocabulary that spans every regulation, not separate reports per standard
- **Machine-readable structure** — the same knowledge base feeds dashboards, agents, and audit tools
- **Change awareness** — when standards update, the ontologies reflect what changed

## How it fits

The ontologies don't generate reports. They provide the structured knowledge that your reporting tools, dashboards, and teams can draw from — consistently, across frameworks.
