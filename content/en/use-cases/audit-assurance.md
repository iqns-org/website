# IQ:NS for Audit / Assurance

Audit and assurance teams need to verify that controls, policies, and AI systems are aligned. IQ:NS provides a structured foundation for evidence, control mappings, and cross-framework coverage.

## The challenge

Auditors often rebuild the same mappings in each review cycle. They face inconsistent terminology, multiple frameworks, and fragmented evidence sources.

## What the ontologies provide

- **Evidence mapping** — connect controls and obligations to the underlying systems and processes
- **Consistent terminology** — one concept links requirements across standards and internal policies
- **Coverage reporting** — see which frameworks are satisfied and where evidence is missing
- **Repeatable assurance** — automated queries turn structured knowledge into review-ready artifacts

## How it fits

IQ:NS does not conduct audits, but it makes them far easier. By providing a common vocabulary, assurance teams can focus on validation instead of reconstruction.
