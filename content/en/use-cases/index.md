## Why a dedicated use-case collection?

IQ:NS is a shared semantic foundation. Each team uses that foundation differently:

- executives need coherent evidence and risk visibility
- legal teams need consistent, auditable obligation mappings
- security teams need a vocabulary for controls and threat models
- engineers need queryable requirements and agent-ready knowledge
- operations need the same structured knowledge in workflows and catalogs

Each use case page explains how IQ:NS connects institutional knowledge, frameworks, and systems so your organization can act from the same source of truth.
