# Banking

Banking ontology for lending, fraud, supervision, and customer decision controls.

## Why this ontology matters

- Captures lending, fraud, customer and supervision semantics for banks.
- Enables consistent model risk controls across financial services.
- Supports responsible decision-making in regulated banking workflows.
