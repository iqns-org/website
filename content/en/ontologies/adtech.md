# AdTech

Advertising ontology for targeting, attribution, consent, and brand safety.

## Why this ontology matters

- Defines targeting, attribution, consent and brand safety in ad systems.
- Helps advertising teams reduce bias and improve transparency.
- Supports interoperable governance for programmatic media workflows.
