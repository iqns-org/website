# Media & Content

Media ontology for synthetic content, disclosure, moderation, and platform trust.

## Why this ontology matters

- Models synthetic content, attribution and moderation semantics.
- Helps media teams improve transparency and platform trust.
- Supports responsible publishing and content governance workflows.
