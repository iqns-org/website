# Health Sector

Health ontology for clinical safety, patient impact, and regulated AI workflows.

## Why this ontology matters

- Encodes clinical safety, patient rights and regulated health workflows.
- Helps care providers adopt AI in a safer, more transparent way.
- Supports interoperability across medical AI systems and compliance.
