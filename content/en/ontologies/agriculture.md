# Agriculture & Food Systems

Agriculture ontology for precision farming, yield models, and food-chain assurance.

## Why this ontology matters

- Models precision farming, traceability and food system governance.
- Helps agricultural teams manage risk and sustainability with AI.
- Supports interoperable supply-chain and yield forecasting workflows.
