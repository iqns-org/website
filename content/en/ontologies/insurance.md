# Insurance

Insurance ontology for underwriting, claims, pricing fairness, and governance.

## Why this ontology matters

- Captures underwriting, claims, pricing, and fairness semantics.
- Helps insurers apply consistent governance across model decisions.
- Supports compliance with financial regulation and customer protection.
