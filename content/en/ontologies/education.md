# Education

Education ontology for admissions, grading, learner rights, and oversight.

## Why this ontology matters

- Captures learner rights, assessment and privacy semantics.
- Helps education systems govern AI-supported admissions and grading.
- Supports fair, transparent learning experiences with shared models.
