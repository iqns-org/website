# AI Sector

Core AI governance ontology for frameworks, controls, risks, and obligations.

## Why this ontology matters

- Core AI governance ontology that makes frameworks and obligations machine-readable.
- Enables consistent risk and control mapping across sectors.
- Supports automated compliance, reporting, and cross-domain alignment.
- Provides a shared foundation for AI policy, controls, and model risk.
