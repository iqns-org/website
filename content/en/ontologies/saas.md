# SaaS Platforms

SaaS ontology for tenant controls, hosted AI products, and platform governance.

## Why this ontology matters

- Defines tenancy, product and platform governance semantics for SaaS AI.
- Helps operators manage shared responsibility and service controls.
- Supports safer integrations and consistent customer-facing behavior.
