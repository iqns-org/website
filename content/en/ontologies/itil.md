# ITIL & Service Management

Service ontology for operations, incidents, changes, and knowledge workflows.

## Why this ontology matters

- Makes service, incident and change workflows machine-readable.
- Supports operational consistency across IT service management teams.
- Helps link AI governance to service and knowledge management practices.
