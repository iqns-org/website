Patient safety improves when diagnosis, procedure, and medication codes mean the same everywhere.

## لماذا هذا مهم

Patient outcomes improve when all systems interpret clinical guidance identically.

## ما يبدو عليه ذلك في الممارسة العملية

- A clinical diagnosis is understood identically by EHR, imaging, pharmacy, and specialists.
- Treatment decisions are safe because clinical guidance applies consistently everywhere.
- Patient records are portable because semantic definitions travel with data.

## كيف تستخدم الفرق ذلك

- connecting clinical data from multiple EHR vendors without semantic loss
- automating clinical decision support across different care settings
- proving clinical quality through consistent outcome measurement

Medical interoperability is solved at the semantic layer, not technically.
